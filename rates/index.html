RATES

Studio Lessons
My studio lessons are my most affordable options, and allow me to use all of my gear and music to make your lesson as educational and engaging as possible! I am conveiniently located near 91st & Memorial.
Individual - No contract, no sign-up fee, simply pay as you go. Payment is due at the time of your lesson. This is a great option for students who aren't sure if lessons are for them yet! $40 for an hour lesson.
Monthly - A flat, monthly rate that guarantees you at least 4 lessons a month. This is perfect for students ready to make a commitment to mastering their instrument. $80 for half-hour lessons and $140 for hour lessons.
Semester - For students who are planning on taking lessons indefinitely, the semester plan lets you pay for three months in advance at a reduced rate. $220 for half-hour lessons and $380 for hour lessons.

Lessons At Your Home
For many students, it's much more conveinient to have their instructor come to them. All you need is a quiet room, a music stand and two chairs to start taking lessons in the comfort of your own home! For busy parents, this service can be a lifesaver. I am currently only offering 45-minute lessons for at-home students.
Individual - Due to the expenses and extra time required to drive to students, I am not currently offering individual lessons outside of the initial free meet-and-greet lesson.
Monthly - $140 for a month of 45-minute lessons.
Semester - $380 for 3 months of 45-minute lessons.

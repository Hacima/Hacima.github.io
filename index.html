My name is Micah Obal, I am a musician and private instructor. I've been offering guitar, bass and piano lessons in Tulsa for more than a decade. I also play in a symphonic rock band, record at my home studio, lead worship at my church, and even do a little programming in my spare time. Teaching, however, is my primary focus.

I teach weekly 30, 45 and 60-minute lessons on guitar, piano and bass. My students range from 10 years old to over 60, from beginners to advanced. Some want to learn country guitar, others classical piano, and others funk bass. I do my best to make sure each and every one of them learn material that furthers their own goals without neglecting technique or good musicianship.
